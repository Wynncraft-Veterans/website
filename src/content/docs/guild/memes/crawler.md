The Crawler was the main antagonist of Return 66, the second interrobang done by the guild. The crawler interrobang story was written by guild member Felixfex and Wenweia. The return revolved around tracking down The Crawler, who had been causing incidents such as wenluck and stealing braincells. During the event, many troubles of the guild members were blamed on The Crawler, such as fruma being delayed or interrupting our Abso Hell event.

In the end, the interrobang return was an april fools joke, and led the guild to a skyblock copy of the guild island!
