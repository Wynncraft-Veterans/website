The guild cults are groups of [VETS] members who follow one of a few different cults. These cults are primarily 'led' by the guild chiefs, whether willingly or unwillingly.

The cult activities came to a climax during Return 45 'Hallowen'. In this return for guild founder Wenweia's birthday, the Metrafish cult (AKA Metracult) and Wenweia cult (AKA Wencult) went to battle with various guild minigames. 

Since then, the system has been partially formalised, with hidden channels accessible by `~joincult` on discord and hidden from current chiefs lest they know what their cults are up to.
