Also referencing "Grim Shady", this meme protests the common perception of grimtrap as being a suboptimal weapon. A reference to a shadestepper build designed and refined by Arxhe_, this build was most notably worn by Minethuselah, who made sure to mention this fact frequently, protesting whenever grimtrap was charactarised as poor or useless.

This meme has immortalised in the guild reliquary, wherein we display the worst Grimtrap roll the guild can obtain at any given time. Guild members are encouraged to come forward with their own Grimtraps if the IDs are worse than the Grimtrap being displayed.
