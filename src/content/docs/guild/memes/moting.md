Moting is the term used when a player is not demoted or promoted, but set to their own rank, AKA 'moting'. This is known to be an extreme action and is only used on the worst possible criminals. 

Once of the most severe cases in [VETS] history was when guild member EenRandomG was using an outdated version of Vetsmod, causing issues with some of the Vetsmod operations. This resulted in a notorious triple mote from 3 chiefs at once, the greatest in history.
