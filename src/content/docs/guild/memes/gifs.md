Question mark based discord commands refer to a series of GIFs that play upon sending their ?name. These feature VETS members in various scenarios that either reference the member themselves, or something about vets culture.

In no particular order, some of these are:

## ?Spin

The guild chief Nazzae is famous for spinning around with his mouse. Because sometimes, you just need to ?spin

## ?Doom

Sometimes, you get tired of spinning... ?doom! When something bad happens, especially if it is a mild irritation, that spells out certain ?doom. Another gif command centred around Nazzae.

## ? Illuminate

Conversely, spinning can be a joyful occasion! For when you are feeling happy and need to express the desire to spin, you can ?Illuminate. Another Nazzae gif.

## ?Stare

Sometimes, especially when encountering a deer, you need to ?stare into someone's soul. This one features the guild chief Holidaze, since it is impossible to win a staring contest against her.

## ?Stonks

Sometimes, people spend all day, every day, looking at the trade market. Such is the case with this one, which is a reference to guild member xrl_11 completing numerous transactions via the trade market, turning to the item identifier, then returning to the market to trade some more.
