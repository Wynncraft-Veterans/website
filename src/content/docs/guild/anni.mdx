<div id="section-overview" class="anni-section active">

**Wynn's only [Major World Event](http://localhost:4321/docs/major-changes/rekindled#major), Anni, is a group boss battle occuring every few days.** <br/>
Occuring at effectively random times, this is a 10 player boss battle announced 8h in advance. We track the event and send out pings in [#annihilation](https://discord.com/channels/1313769181321236490/1339393368672702567), which you can [subscribe](https://discord.com/channels/1313769181321236490/1318077157938954270/1458149624924733521) to.

The VETS anni system is designed to be as inclusive as possible, with a focus on helping returning players get involved and learn the mechanics. 
**If you are a member of the in-game guild (or on the waitlist), we will do our best to get you a slot in the event and ensure you have the support needed to succeed.** <small>This is usually a guarantee, but our capacity is <abbr title="Click this for an explanatory popup!"><button type="button" popovertarget="anni-capacity-popover" class="underline bg-transparent border-0 p-0 m-0 cursor-pointer text-inherit">limited</button></abbr></small>.

	<h3>Capacity Limits</h3>
	<p>Guild Annihilation parties consist of multiple parties spread across multiple worlds: each party has a wynn-imposed limit of 10 users. Before filling a party, we make sure to assign it a composition we are confident can succeed; generally, this means a <em>minimum</em> of 5 users with highly capable builds.</p>
	
	<p><strong>Sometimes, we do not have enough builds to form enough parties to fit everyone, and other times, we have enough builds but the core number is insufficiently divisible.</strong> In these cases, we have to make the difficult decision to limit the number of attendees as to ensure that the parties we do run retain a high chance of success.</p>

	<p>Finally, we also have to consider the practical limitations of creating parties, giving people time to get ready, and ensuring everyone fits on a world.
	Even in cases where enough people show up later to have allowed us to split into more parties (and thus handle more people), we may simply not have the time to do so.</p>

	<blockquote>
		<p><strong>TLDR:</strong> We are always working to improve our capacity, and will do our best to guarantee a slot, but sometimes circumstances may prevent us from adding you to a party.
		We thank you for your understanding.</p>
	</blockquote>
</div>

In the case of extra slots, friends from the wider wynnvets community are welcome to fill them.

### Joining Your Party
**Once you are online, please wait for staff to invite you to one of our parties!**<br/>
On quiet days, parties are usually formed 60mins before the event; on busy days, you may need to wait up to 40mins, with parties being formed as late as 20mins before the event.

**Even if parties are not yet announced, please remain online and attuned to chat!**<br/>
You can do other things while you wait, but please be ready to switch over once the invites go out! It's also a good idea to have some [potions](https://wynncraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Stat_Potion) ready from your highest level town!

### 15 Mins Before
**Once you are in your party, your party's staff leader will assign you a role based on what you indicated on AnniRoles and the party's needs!**
At least 15 mins before the event, please collect your [potions](https://wynncraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Stat_Potion), switch to your build, and head to the portal (`313 -1289`)!

### 5 Mins Before
**At least 5 before (usually well earlier), your party leader will announce a scroll spot and its coordinates. Be sure to stand there when it gets announced!**

(The guild provides consumable [scroll buffs](http://wynnvets.org/docs/major-changes/economy#types-of-crafted-items) that will be used by your party leader 30 seconds before the event starts.
You need to be within 3 blocks of them to get these effects; once the scrolls have been used, feel free to jump down and into the world event arena)

		section.classList.add('active');
		if (scrollTarget) {
			requestAnimationFrame(function() {
				scrollTarget.scrollIntoView({ behavior: 'instant', block: 'start' });
			});
		} else {
			window.scrollTo({ top: 0, behavior: 'instant' });
		}
	}

		var container = document.getElementById(containerId);
		if (!container) return;
		var tab = document.getElementById(tabId);

	function resolveHash(hash) {
		if (!hash) return null;

		/* Check tab hashes first (these are virtual - no DOM element with this id) */
		var tabInfo = allTabHashes[hash];
		if (tabInfo) {
			var container = document.getElementById(tabInfo.container);
